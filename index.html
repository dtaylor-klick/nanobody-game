About BRAND O
Precision is the point.

Next-generation nanobodies consist of one or more domains based on the small antigen-binding variable regions of heavy-chain-only antibodies (VHH). Nanobody technology has a number of advantages over traditional antibodies, including their small size and enhanced tissue penetration.
